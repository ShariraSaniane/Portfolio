IRRIGATION POINT

“Irrigation Point: Smart Irrigation in Delta City” application.

  • Technology: Figma
  • Client: Society

Featured Project


Background

Irrigation is essential for agriculture, providing supplemental water to crops. In Indonesia, 74.1% of agricultural water needs are met through irrigation. Irrigation systems rely on rivers, lakes, and groundwater. The management of irrigation is guided by principles of sustainability, equity, and transparency.

Solution

“Irrigation Point: Smart Irrigation in Delta City” application. This application trial will be centered on the city of Sidoarjo using IoT which functions to carry out accurate measurements, enabling accurate measurements, precise control, and efficient irrigation management based on data collected from connected sensors. .
